Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 4, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to lovterest. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome Extension handles your data. We built this extension with a strict privacy-first approach: we do not collect, store, or share your personal data.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

In compliance with the Google Chrome Web Store Policies, we explicitly state that:

  • We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII).
  • We do not track your browsing history outside of Pinterest.com.
  • We do not log your interactions, clicks, or the pins you like.
  • We do not use analytics trackers or third-party tracking scripts.

3. How the Extension Works (Local Data Only)

The lovterest extension operates entirely within your browser:

  • Storage: The extension uses Chrome's local storage solely to save your preference of whether the extension is enabled or disabled, and to temporarily cache the API endpoint required to perform the "Love" action.
  • Network Requests: The extension communicates directly and only with Pinterest's official servers (pinterest.com) on your behalf when you interact with a Pin. No data is ever routed through any external third-party servers.

4. Permissions We Require

  • storage: Required to save your extension toggle state.
  • *://*.pinterest.com/*: Required to overlay the custom heart button and interact with the Pinterest interface.

5. Third-Party Sharing

Because we do not collect any user data, we have absolutely no data to sell, trade, or rent to third parties.
